Our Services

We offer an array of core services, some of which include:

Risk Assessment

Predict the fire risk and assess the local weather and vegetation conditions
  • Fire danger index: Access a 9-day forecast and detailed mapping of the fire risk.
  • Weather information: Stay informed about critical factors including wind speed and direction, temperature, lightning strikes, and humidity.
  • Vegetation & terrain analysis: Assess conditions using land cover, fuel maps, vegetation indices, and 3D maps.

Early Detection

Receive instant alerts of active wildfires via different notification methods.
  • Data from over 27 satellites: Get precise 24/7 detection with multiple updates per hour, capturing fires as small as 4×4 meters.
  • Automatic notifications: Receive instant email, WhatsApp messages, SMS, or in-app alerts within minutes of new fire detections in your monitored areas.
  • Accurate location & fire area: Get precise location coordinates and estimates of the affected area

Fire Spread Simulation

Precise 24/7 monitoring with several updates per hour
  • Live fire tracking: Monitor the active fire front and its development in near real-time.
  • Fire propagation: Benefit from our analysis of crucial fire drivers such as weather, topography, and fuel to estimate the potential fire spread.
  • Incident management: coordinate effective actions for fire management and combat.

Damage Analysis

Quantify the burnt area automatically and estimate losses.
  • Burnt area: Utilize advanced technology for high-resolution mapping of burnt areas and severity assessment.
  • Long-term fire statistics: Uncover patterns and trends in wildfire activity using our comprehensive historical dataset, automatic dashboards, and visualizations.
  • Fire origin & evolution: Track the starting point of a fire and observe its progression over time.
